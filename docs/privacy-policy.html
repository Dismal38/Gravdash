Privacy Policy

GravShift — Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the GravShift mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. GravShift is a fully offline arcade game. It does not require an account, does not contact any remote server, and does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

  • No name, email, phone number, or contact information is collected.
  • No location, device identifiers, or advertising IDs are collected.
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are transmitted off-device.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

The App stores a small amount of data locally on your device only, using the standard application storage provided by Android. This data never leaves your device. It includes:

  • Your chosen player name (if you set one).
  • Your local high score.
  • Audio and haptics preferences.

You can erase all locally stored data at any time by clearing the App's storage from your device's Settings or by uninstalling the App.

3. Permissions

GravShift requests only the minimum Android permissions required for gameplay (such as vibration for haptic feedback). It does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, files, or location.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

5. Third-Party Services

GravShift does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or social SDKs. The App is distributed through the Google Play Store, which has its own privacy policy that applies to your use of the store itself.

6. Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data to be at risk. Locally stored gameplay data is protected by your device's standard application sandboxing.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
