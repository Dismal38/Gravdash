## What's a good launch week target?

For a $0.99 paid arcade with **no marketing budget** that gets approved
by Play Store review:

| Metric | Day 1 | Week 1 |
|---|---|---|
| Page views | 30–80 | 200–600 |
| Installs | 1–5 | 10–40 |
| Wishlist | 5–15 | 30–80 |
| Reviews | 0–2 | 3–10 |

These are real numbers — paid apps without marketing convert 3–8% of
page views to installs. The way to push past these:

1. Post the **share-card PNG** the game already generates to TikTok /
   X / Reddit (`r/AndroidGaming`, `r/IndieDev`, `r/incremental_games`).
2. Reach out to indie arcade YouTubers (search "best one-tap games 2026").
3. Submit to **Indie Game** subreddits and Discord communities.
4. Run a **48-hour free promotion** in week 2 to seed reviews.
